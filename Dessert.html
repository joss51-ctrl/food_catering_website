Indonesian Cuisine

KleponRp 15,000

Klepon is a traditional Indonesian sweet snack made from glutinous rice flour stuffed with palm sugar and coated in grated coconut. It has chewy texture and delightful burst of liquid palm sugar inside.

Ice GempolRp 20,000

Consists of small, round dumplings made from rice flour. These dumplings are then mixed with palm sugar syrup and coconut milk, creating a sweet and creamy dessert.

KetanRp 20,000

A traditional Indonesian dessert made from black glutinous rice, coconut milk, palm sugar, and pandan leaves served cold with additional coconut milk poured on top.

Ice CendolRp 20,000

It consists of translucent green jelly noodles made from tapioca flour, flavored with pandan leaves, and cooked in coconut milk and palm sugar syrup.

Western Cuisine

Caramel PudingRp 30,000

Made from a custard-like mixture of eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. It has creamy texture, rich flavor, and the contrast between the sweet caramel and the delicate custard.

Apple PieRp 45,000

It consists of a flaky pastry crust filled with sliced apples, sugar, and cinnamon. It is beloved for its sweet, fruity flavor and comforting, homely aroma.

Matcha Crepe Rp 28,000

made from thin sheets of batter consisting of flour, eggs, milk, and matcha powder filled with cream cheese and whipped cream. It has a unique flavor from the matcha powder.

OhurosRp 23,000

Made from a simple dough of flour, water, and salt. Fried until golden brown. Coated in a mixture of sugar and cinnamon, served warm and dipped in chocolate sauce.
