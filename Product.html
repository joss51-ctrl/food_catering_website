Indonesian Cuisine

Gado-GadoRp 25,000

Gado-gado is a traditional Indonesian dish consisting of a colorful mix of vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and hard-boiled eggs, all served with a generous helping of peanut sauce. garnished with prawn crackers

PempekRp 35,000

Pempek is made from a mixture of mackerel fish and tapioca starch. Served with a tangy, sweet, and spicy vinegar-based sauce known as cuko made from palm sugar, vinegar, garlic, chili, and a dash of salt.

Nasi KuningRp 20,000

Nasi Kuning is made by cooking rice with coconut milk and turmeric, which gives it its distinctive yellow color. Served with fried chicken, fried eggs, fried tempeh, and sambal.

Chicken SatayRp 40,000

Chicken Satay is made from skewered and pieces of chicken marinated in a mixture of spices such as turmeric, garlic and lemongrass, served with a flavorful peanut sauce for dipping.

Western Cuisine

SpagetthiRp 35,000

Boiled in salted water until al dente served with a carbonara sauce (creamy sauce with eggs, cheese). Garnished with grated cheese, fresh herbs, and prawn.

Tomahawk SteakRp 70,000

Tomahawk steak is a large, bone-in ribeye steak. Seasoned with salt, pepper, and other spices before being seared on a hot grill or cast-iron skillet to develop a flavorful crust.

Fish&ChipRp 55,000

Deep fried cod fish served with chunky fried potatoes. Served tartar sauce (a mayonnaise-based sauce with chopped pickles), and a wedge of lemon.

Cordon BleuRp 55,000

Cordon bleu made from flattened chicken breast that is rolled or folded around a filling of ham and cheese and fried until golden and crispy. Served with mash potato and mayonnaise
