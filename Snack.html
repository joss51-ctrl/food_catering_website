Indonesian Cuisine

Sweet MartabakRp 35,000

Sweet Martabak is a popular Indonesian sweet treat that is essentially a thick pancake or crepe filled with chocolate sprinkles, cheese, condensed milk, and crushed peanuts.

Chicken SempolanRp 25,000

Chicken Sempolan is a traditional Indonesian dish, it consists of chicken pieces, typically marinated in a blend of local spices and herbs, then deep-fried until crispy and golden brown.

Tahu Gejrot Rp 15,000

Tahu Gejrot is a popular Indonesian street food. It consists of fried tofu cubes that are served with a sweet and tangy sauce made from palm sugar, vinegar, garlic, shallots, chili, and tamarind.

Spicy CilokRp 10,000

Cilok is an Indonesian street food snack that is made from tapioca flour mixed with water and other flavorings to form a dough-like consistency. Sserved with spicy peanut sauce, sweet soy sauce, and fried shallots.
